KIPP vs IDEA — Florida Proficiency Trends, 2021–2026

Six-year history of % proficient (Level 3+) by network, with year-over-year growth. Companion to the 2026 snapshot dashboard. Enrollment-weighted across all campuses, grades 3–8. Source: Florida DOE school-level results.

Read the 2022→2023 jump with care: 2021–2022 used the FSA; Florida switched to the FAST / B.E.S.T. assessment in 2023, so part of that one-year change reflects the test change, not only school performance. 2021 was also a COVID-depressed year. IDEA campuses phased in over 2022–2024, so early years are blank for networks that didn't yet exist.

% proficient over time —

Each point is the enrollment-weighted % of students at Level 3 or above. Gaps = network/campuses not yet open that year.

Year-over-year change (percentage points) —

Bars show the change in % proficient vs the prior year. Green = improvement, red = decline.
